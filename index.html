Background

The US is one of the most obese and overweight countries in the world. This visualization will explore the breakdown of obesity by demographic, in addition to the trends over time.
The data can be found at https://stateofchildhoodobesity.org/monitor/. Data from the CDC can be found at https://www.cdc.gov/nchs/data-visualization/mortality-leading-causes/index.htm

Top 10 Leading Causes of Deaths (2017)

Almost all of the causes of death shown above have obesity as a risk factor.In particular, obesity contributes tremendously to your chances of contracting the two largest causes of death in the US: cancer and heart disease.
